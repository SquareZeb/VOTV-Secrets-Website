Achievements

Description:

In VOTV, exists many achievements that mark the player's accomplishments and as well as progress. Some achievements are hidden or unobtainable, while some can be earned throughout the player's gameplay. Then there are advancements which only exist within the playable saves as opposed to the main data save of the game.
