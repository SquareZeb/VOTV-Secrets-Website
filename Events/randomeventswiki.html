Random Events

Description:

A lot of the events in VOTV are scripted story events, however some events that exist in the game are either not scripted but rather are completely random.

Event Description Rarity Image
Black Fog Event A creepy noise can be heard from across the map, soon the world will begin to grow darker and foggier with each second passing until it eventually becomes so dark and so foggy that you can't see 20 meters in front of you. This event usually lasts 3-4 minutes and isn't very threatening. Common
Red Sky Event At night the sky will turn completely bright red instead of the usual dark black color and it will stay like this for some time until it eventually disappears. Uncommon
Gears Event 7-12 giant gears can be seen and heard from across the map, these gears are animated and seen spinning around, however they have no collision and are stationary. This lasts for some time before the giant gears disappear. Very Rare
Ghost Graffiti Event A drawing or graffiti will show up on a base of a random satellite dish. Common
Chair Skeleton Event After walking and exploring for a while and upon returning back to base, you may encounter a a bunch of bone props that is organized and spawned in a skeleton shape and is sitting on the main chair. You can take apart the bones, however do note that the skull cannot be used for the Hell Opening Event. Common
Jellyfish Event A bunch of jellyfish will spawn and begin flying in the sky, they will do this for a long time before eventually flying out of bounds. Rare
Roar Event In the older versions of VOTV, the roar event is supposed to signify the end of the game and demo completion, this was usually triggered upon day 35. But since then in the newer versions the event appears to be completely random. It may have a chance of appearing on day 48-50 in storymode but its very unlikely. Super Rare
Possessed Kerfus Event Kerfus despite being off will move on its own in a random location and after some time will drop a ball of flesh on the ground. Extremely Rare
Light Tube Event A light tube prop with a bright blue light emitting will spawn high above player, randomly in any part of the forest. It will quickly fall down and shatter, you can attempt to catch the prop and keep it as extra light. Very Rare
Pink Wisps Event Pink Wisp Entities will spawn all over the world. Rare
Flesh Rain Event A loud shaking roar will be heard all over the world, then the flesh mesh balls will start dropping for 1-2 minutes all around but will consolidate around the player. This gives the player tons of free flesh. Rare
Mannequin Disappearance Event All mannequins in your base will disappear. However, they can all be found laying in the small patch of gravel near the Uniform Satellite Dish. Uncommon
Mannequin Moving Event One per day, mannequins will first take off their standing pole and begin moving around. They usually move towards the player and while they don't do anything initially, if they are lit on fire they will begin attacking, hurting, and knocking down the player. Uncommon
Mannequin Reorganize Event A posed mannequin will be blocking the main entrance hallway, another posed mannequin looking through Kel's room window , and 6 cups stacked on each other either on the table or on the floor. Rare
Blue Light Prank Event All power switches will be switched off just like Poweroff Event, however this time there will be extremely bright light blue tubes lighting certain parts of the base. The entity radar will detect these as entities, however they are extremely fragile so be very careful when handling them. Common
Password Attempt Event Throughout the day, you may hear or encounter a mysterious invisible force attempting to guess the password to the keylocks. Rare
Broken Vent Event The vent either above the Signal room or Kel's room will break open and fall off. Rare
Ena's Kindness Event Ena will send you some crossiants and a supportive note. Rare
Misplaced Sushi Event A box of sushi and a note detailing its accidental delivery. Rare
Radiotower Disappearance Event The Radiotower Structure will disappear completely for a day or two. Rare
Grunge Oils Event Spills of oil will spawn in front of the main entrance doorway, Kel's room patio doorway, below Radiotower base, and the garage doorway. Rare
Security Breach On the command terminal, you will notice some random user typing out commands, supposed trying to shut something down. Luckily, none of the commands work and the user eventually leaves. Rare
Furfur Locker Head When opening a locker anywhere in the Main Base, there is a chance a Furfur head can pop out and jumpscare. Rare
Cursed Mirror Base Event While in Main Base, there is a very small chance that you'll be teleported outside the barrier and map into a another version of the Main Base, however very dark and foggy with no doors or props anywhere. As soon as you fall off the parking lot you get teleported back into the main map. Extremely Rare
