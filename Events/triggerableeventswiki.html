Triggerable Events

Description:

A very few amount of events in VOTV can be triggered manually either by the player doing something specific or following a specific action.

Event Description Triggers Image
Rozital Ship Event At midnight, a bright and very loud purple laser will be shot down from above hitting the Ship Engine Object, it will do this for 30 seconds. The Rozital that is showcased here is looking for a lost ship engine that is either misplaced or moved. After finding it, the Rozital shoots down a purple beam. The Rozitals for being an advanced race is most likely trying to protect their technology from humans. You must obtain the Lifecrystal Signal, process it to level, and send it off to the researchers. From there you will need to wait until midnight.
Ship Engine Event The Ship Engine Object will begin rotating it's gears, once it locks up it begins to hover and fly off the 2 rocks, and soon it slowly builds up a purple energy that eventually climaxes at a large bright purple explosion. This event is linked to the Rozital Ship Event, after the Rozital fires the purple beam at the object its charged. Once fully charged either the object disappears or is destroyed, presumably the Rozital having accomplished its mission. You must perform or trigger the Rozital Ship Event in order to trigger this one.
Hell Opening Event The 7 skulls will begin moving around slightly, eventually with enough time they will pick up the pace and start moving around quickly in a circular motion. Afterwards, with enough momentum they will begin floating until all 7 of them hit a specific point. With enough time passed a black hole will spawn in, sucking in all of the skulls. The black hole's gravitional pull and size will increase with every second passed. If you were to fall into or be pulled into the black hole you will be teleported into untitled_9999 world. If you were to stay as far away as possible and wait out the black hole which will take a couple of minutes to despawn, the Unnamed Rock Object will be spawned for you to have access to. You must collect all 7 skulls and place them in the very darkened spot of the Stonehenge. You must perform or trigger the Rozital Ship Event and Ship Engine Event in order to obtain the 7th skull which is located underneath the Ship Engine Object.
The Evil Event In the span of a few milliseconds, the entire world will be overlayed with the evil texture and abruptly end your game, give the worst ending, and game over. The Evil manages to transport itself or at least a seed of it through the signal that you accept. From there, it immediately converts everything within the immediate area. Soon it infests and converts everything on Earth instantly. You must obtain the Evil Signal, process it to level 3, send it off to the researchers, and wait some time.
Bad Sun Event The sun is censored with a black square, the natural brightness of the world increases significantly, and if you step outside in the range of the censored sun's rays you will take damage. The event lasts for the entire duration of the day until the sun goes down and it is night. Can be triggered on the 24th day of any real life month and it's also random.
Undead Deer Event The Undead Deer Entity will effectively replace the normal Deer Entity and a dead deer corpse can be found anywhere. If you eat all of it you are able to despawn the Undead Deer Entity for good. Must obtain the Deer Signal.
Shorts Arir Tp Event A bright cyan flash will flash the whole world, then a stasis chamber of some kind will spawn in valley near the Fence Opening Landmark. It will drop two decapitated Ariral Legs as well as the Gravity Gun. There's also the shorts society logo which you can g Must obtain the ShortarirTp Signal.
Virus Event All the screens on all the terminals will flip a red skull and effectively be glitching out which would then result in the entire base losing all its power. From there you would wait a couple seconds before you can flip the switches again. You'll also recieve an email giving you strong a critique of your training. Must obtain the Virus Signal.
Shit Duende Event If you were to enter into the bathroom you will notice the Shit Duende Entity sitting on the toilet before it phases out of existence. Must obtain the Shitduende Signal.
Howl Event You can hear a loud howl coming from some unknown entity or origin. Walk to these exact approximate coordinates (X: -345, Y: -103).
Abandoned Well Sleep Event At the bottom of the Abandoned Well, as soon as your sleep meter reaches 0 and your character attempts to sleep the game will teleport you into the untitled_129. Completely zero out your sleep at the bottom.
Ritual Event After placing all four runes into the correct places and stabbing yourself with the ritual knife, a Lambert Plush will spawn on top of the altar for you to grab. You need to obtain all four runes that include fire, water, earth, and air. After obtaining them and warping over to the Ritual Altar, place all of the runes in the correct spots then stab yourself using the knife provided on the altar.
Log Event Once through the log, you will teleport to the other log that is similar to the one you just entered. You are able to effectively teleport between two completely different points on the map like walking through a portal. You have to walk through an any angled log that is sitting on top of some rocks at 6:00 am.
Blood Skeleton Event A bloodied skeleton with corroded bones will spawn and spill some blood on the ground. A metallic device known as the Alien Thing will spawn in the ribcage or fall out. Must obtain the Process Signal, afterwards it will be located near TR_0 Transformer.
Halloween Event An entirely seperate gamemode that lasts for a few days on the real life date of October 31st and onward. Features a red colored world with 13 glowing pumpkins and multiple entities that rapidly spawn. Some of these entities which are considered "Halloween Special" include: Black Fogling, Insomnia Demon, and Furfur. If there more of this kind, they will be added to later versions of the Halloween Special. The goal is for you to collect all 13 glowing pumpkins which change throughout Halloween Special versions to trigger the ending. The ending which has the player collect one signal, download, and save it which will trigger or spawn in the SKELEO Entity. Occurs on the 31st of October and a few days after in real life time.
Tardis Event A reference to the Tardis Box from Doctor Who, the actual box itself will spawn in front of the main base and after a minute will disappear. Must obtain the Tardis Signal.
Wolfgang Awakening Event The Wolfgang entity will run around looking for any pumpkins, during that time the barrier inside AB Cave will unlock. Must place down pumpkin near AB Cave.
Furfur Plush Event The Furfur Plush will spawn right underneath the Furfur Paltar Object. You need to obtain all three Furfur Paltar Pieces and assemble them, then warping over to Ritual Altar, you will place the object on top of the table. From, there taking a knife, grab one gore gib and burn it. While it is burning place it underneath the bridge of the object.
