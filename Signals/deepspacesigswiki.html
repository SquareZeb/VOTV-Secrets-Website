Deep Space Signals

Description:

A lot of the signals in VOTV will be signals with an image of just outer space and it may not seem much, heck even the properties can tell the same story. But don't be fooled, some of these "empty" signals have some meaning or could hide some very interesting surprises.

For each signal audio sound there will be a little button click or notification, that is to signify the start of the signal audio.
